Our Story

A Century of
Quiet Perfection.

Since 1924, NOIR has defined the art of refined hospitality. Born from the vision of architect Édouard Lemarque, our hotel stands as a monument to understated grandeur — its original marble floors still cold beneath your feet at dawn.

Each room carries a story. Each corridor breathes history. Every element — from the hand-pressed linens to the single-origin coffee — is a deliberate act of care that no algorithm can replicate.
